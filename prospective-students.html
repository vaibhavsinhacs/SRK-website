Information for Prospective Students

I am currently looking for a post-doctoral fellow and Ph.D. Research Scholars, primarily in the broad areas of Guidance and Control of Autonomous Vehicles, with special focus on Cooperative Control of Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) or Cooperative Active Aircraft Defense. However, the research topics are not restricted to only these and can be decided based on mutual interest. Interested applicants may contact me through email, along with their CVs.
